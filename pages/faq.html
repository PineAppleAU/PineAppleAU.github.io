Product Information

What technology is used in The PineApple Vision glasses?

Our AR glasses are equipped with 4K micro-OLED displays, advanced AR Quad-Core processors, and optics designed in-house for a broad field of view. They are the pinnacle of AR technology, providing an immersive and crisp visual experience in a lightweight form factor.

What is the battery life of PineApple Vision S?

The PineApple Vision S offers up to 8 hours of battery life on a single charge under typical usage conditions, while the base model provides approximately 5 hours.

Is PineApple Vision suitable for outdoor activities?

Yes, PineApple Vision has been designed for both indoor and outdoor activities with adaptive display technology for different lighting conditions.

Can I use PineApple Vision with my prescription glasses?

Currently, PineApple Vision is not compatible with prescription glasses. However, prescription lens options will be available in future models.

Troubleshooting

What should I do if my Vision won't turn on?

Firstly, ensure your Vision is fully charged. If they still won't turn on, perform a hard reset by holding down the power button (the center button located on the front of the frame) for 10 seconds. If issues persist, contact our support team for assistance.

How can I improve tracking accuracy in my living space?

For optimal tracking, ensure your environment is well-lit and has distinct visual features that the glasses can detect. Avoid plain walls and empty spaces. If necessary, recalibrate the tracking through the settings menu.

My Vision is overheating, what can I do?

Unlike computers and other high power devices, the Vision utilises passive cooling to draw heat away from the CPU, however this isnt as efficient as active cooling methods. As such, overheating can result if operated in hot climates or for long periods of time. If your glasses are overheating, take a break from using them to allow them to cool down. Avoid direct sunlight and high-temperature environments. If overheating occurs frequently, contact our customer service.

How do I update my Vision to the latest firmware?

Updates are delivered 'over-the-air' to your AR glasses. Ensure they are connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data and accept the update notification, or check for updates manually in the settings menu.
