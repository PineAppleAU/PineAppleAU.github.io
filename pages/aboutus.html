Our Journey

2016 - A Visionary Beginning

PineApple's story began in the heart of Newcastle, NSW, born from a group of visionaries who dared to ask, "What if reality could be augmented?" From a small, dynamic team of engineers and designers, PineApple embarked on a mission to create a groundbreaking product. The early days were filled with brainstorming sessions, prototype development, and a relentless pursuit of a technology that could seamlessly blend the digital with the physical.

2017 - Crafting the Future

This year marked PineApple's strategic dive into intensive research and development. Our team explored cutting-edge optics, user interface designs, and compact, powerful processing solutions. The goal was to lay a robust foundation for the Vision AR glasses, ensuring they would be both revolutionary in function and form.

2018 - Prototype Unveiled

The unveiling of our first prototype at a renowned tech convention was a watershed moment. The prototype, though in its early stages, showcased groundbreaking features such as high-definition holographic displays and intuitive gesture controls. The response from the tech community was overwhelmingly positive, affirming our belief in the potential of AR in everyday life.

2019 - Enhancing the Vision

Building on the momentum, 2019 was a year of refinement and enhancement. We focused on improving the ergonomics and wearability of the Vision AR glasses, ensuring they were as comfortable as they were technologically advanced. This period also saw the integration of AI-driven features to provide a more personalized user experience.

2020 - Commitment to Sustainability

Our progress in technology was matched by a deepening commitment to sustainability. We embraced green manufacturing processes and began sourcing materials from eco-friendly suppliers. This year, PineApple set an industry standard by introducing recyclable packaging and a device recycling program for our products.

2021 - Global Expansion

The anticipation for PineApple Vision glasses reached a crescendo in 2021. We expanded our reach globally, launching in over 30 countries. This expansion was not just about selling products; it was about cultivating a global community of AR enthusiasts and empowering them with technology that could change the way they see the world.

September 2022 - The PineApple Vision Launch

The launch of the PineApple Vision in September 2022 was a celebration of years of hard work and innovation. The Vision model featured advanced AR capabilities like real-time translation, augmented reality navigation, and immersive gaming experiences. It appealed not only to tech enthusiasts but also to a wider audience, including educators, designers, and everyday consumers seeking to enrich their lives with AR.

May 2023: The Vision S Unveiled

With the overwhelming success of the Vision, we introduced its more sophisticated sibling, the Vision S, in May 2023. Tailored for the luxury market, the Vision S boasted enhanced features like a sleeker design, superior display resolution, and exclusive content partnerships with leading AR developers. This model set a new benchmark in the AR industry, offering unparalleled luxury and performance.

2023 & Beyond - Innovating for Tomorrow

Looking ahead, PineApple remains at the forefront of AR technology, continuously innovating and exploring uncharted territories. Our future plans include expanding our AR ecosystem, introducing new forms of content interaction, and collaborating with various industries to explore novel applications of AR. We remain committed to our mission of making augmented reality an integral part of everyday life, always pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
